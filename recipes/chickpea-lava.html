Chickpea Lava

Description

This was an absolute experience to eat. Made by a culinarily inept flatmate, this is a perfect winter warmer.

If you don't like spicy food, dont even bother with this. If you do like spicy food, also dont bother with this.

You will need:

  • Chickpeas
  • Can of tomatoes
  • ALL OF THE CHILLI
  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Curry powder

Cooking instructions

  1. Chop the onion into tiny little chunks
  2. Heat a pan on medium-high heat until it is pretty hot. Then add some oil of your choice. Flavour does not matter for this dish.
  3. Add the onions and fry until translucent.
  4. Drain and rinse a can of chickpeas, then add to the onions
  5. Immediately follow this with the can of tomatoes and a few hearty tablespoons of curry powder.
  6. Mix this all together until it looks like it's mixed.
  7. This is the important step. Add at least 1 handful of fresh chili, or 2 table spoons of chili powder, preferably both, and mix until combined.
  8. While the "flavours" are being forcibly sold on the slave trade market, add some rice to a rice cooker and cook.
  9. Once the rice is cooked, serve as you would a normal meal, and enjoy.
